My First Week At AkiraChix

The first time I stepped foot into AkiraChix, I received a warm reception from everyone, the administration,alumni and surbodinates included.

Organization

Everything was quite organized and in place just waiting for us

Made New Friends

I got to meet new people from many other countries such as Burundi, Uganda and Ethiopia

Numerous Rules

Rules at AkiraChix are quite many, I have to know how to spread my bed and fold my clothes according to the set standards.

Good Things I Experienced

These are some of the good things I experienced

  3. The food is really delicious
  4. The trainers teach really well.

Things I Did Not Like
